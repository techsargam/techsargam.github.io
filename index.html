DOT

About DOT

DOT Application is a platform for where people can make emergency blood request. The app's been made such that the people are notified in case someone posts the blood request. One can customize the area of interest and the notifications are sent to the app holders. It is difficult to find the right people at the right time when there is a need of blood donor. This application reduces the gap between donor and receiver when such situation arises.

A RESTAURANT WEBSITE
Food Garden

About Food Garden Site

This is a restaurant website for a local restaurant 'Food Garden'. It has been heavily customized as per the needs of the client. Made with MERN stack, this website covers interactive UI and a reliable CMS (Content Management System). This site covers all three branches of the same brand. The admin user (restaurant owner) can add/modify/remove almost every content from menu items to contact info. Furthermore, this site provides two varieties of admin access. Normal admin accesses are provided to the branch managers and a super admin access to the restaurant owner.
